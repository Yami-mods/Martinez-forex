What is Forex Trading?

Foreign Exchange Market

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the world's largest financial market where currencies are traded 24/5. With a daily trading volume exceeding $6.6 trillion, it offers incredible opportunities for traders worldwide.

Key Features:

• 24-Hour Market: Trade Monday to Friday, around the clock
• High Liquidity: Easy entry and exit from positions
• Leverage Trading: Control larger positions with smaller capital
• Currency Pairs: Trade major, minor, and exotic pairs

Major Currency Pairs:

EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, USD/CHF, AUD/USD, USD/CAD, NZD/USD
